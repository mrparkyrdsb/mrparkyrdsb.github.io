Grade 10 Computer Science ICD2O

Prerequisite: None

Recommended for students with zero computer backgrounds in Grade 9 and 10

Content: Intro to Java and Computer Hardware

  • Mark Scheme:
  • Term (70%)
  • Hardware Project 1 (15%)
  • Programming Project 2 (15%)
  • No exam

Grade 11 Computer Science ICS3U

Prerequisite: None | ICD2O is not required.

Recommended for pre-IB students who can't take IB CS and students who want to take ICS4U (all grades are welcome!)

Content: Java Programming and Computer Hardware

  • Mark Scheme:
  • Term (70%)
  • Project (15%)
  • Exam (15% | 1.5 Hours)

Grade 12 Computer Science ICS4U

Prerequisite: ICS3U

Content: Python Programming, Algorithms, Object Oriented Programming and Applications of Computer Science

  • Mark Scheme:
  • Term (70%)
  • Semester Capstone Project (15%)
  • Exam (15% | 1.5 Hours)

IB Computer Science (SL) TEJ3M7 + TEJ4M7

Prerequisite: IB Student | Group 4

Duration: 2 Semesters

Content: System Fundamentals, Computer Organization, Networks, Computational Thinking, and Programming

  • Mark Scheme:
  • Paper 1 (45%)
  • Paper 2 (25%)
  • Internal Assessment (30%)
